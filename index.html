About A Dance With Finance

Christina is not your traditional money expert. They studied Aerospace Engineering in school and have explored many different jobs before realizing their love of helping others with money. Christina decided to join a financial firm in 2019, and worked very hard to learn everything she could about all different aspects of financial planning. Eventually they came to realize their favorite ways of helping people with money were around mindset, organizing and saving money, and that a financial firm was not the best place to do that. so she left in november of 2021 to start A Dance With Finance, a money coaching and educational company! in the last year, A Dance With Finance has supported my people individually and via group courses get financially organized and start saving for their goals with more confidence and less fear! Our courses and coaching are perfect for people who are wanting to heal their relationship to money so they can build confidence arround their money choices and live more in allignment with their goals and values.

Christina (pronouns they/them or she/her) is a hispanic, plus sized, ADHD, agender, dancer who is passionate about many things. they are most passionate about dance and helping other live their best lives, and money is a major tool for living your best life! Christina loves to help others figure out how to make their dreams come true, and has a ton of knowledge about the financial system that can help support those dreams. whrn she's not coaching, she's social dancing, training for performances, teaching dance, hanging out with friends or just relaxing at home.
